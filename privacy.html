Privacy Policy for SEO Analyzer Pro

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Overview

SEO Analyzer Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to provide SEO analysis locally within your browser. This policy explains how we handle information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), browsing history, or sensitive data to any external servers.

Local Processing: All analysis of meta tags, headings, and site structure is performed entirely on your local machine. No data leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The extension requires access to the "active tab" to read the HTML structure of the page you are currently viewing. This is used solely to generate the SEO report for that specific page.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.
